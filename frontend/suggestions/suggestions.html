Suggestions for Reducing Carbon Footprint

Ecobyte Datacounter Extension

Download our browser extension that automatically tracks carbon dioxide consumption while browsing the internet.

Alternative Search Engine

Ecosia is a search engine that plants trees with the revenue generated from searches. It is ecological, transparent, and uses renewable energy.

Gift a Tree

Treedom is a platform that allows you to plant trees to combat climate change and support local communities. Every tree planted is tracked online.

Green Thumb

If you have a garden, you can plant trees and plants to offset CO2 emissions. You can also plant indoor plants to improve air quality.

Regular Maintenance

Perform periodic cleaning of your email inbox to eliminate spam and old emails, reducing CO2 consumption.
